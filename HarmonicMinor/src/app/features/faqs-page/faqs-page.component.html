Returns & Refunds

What is the return policy?

We accept returns within 14 days of delivery, as long as the item is unused, in its original packaging, and in resalable condition. Proof of purchase is required. Return shipping costs are the customer's responsibility unless the product is defective or incorrect.

Are refunds available?

We offer refunds within 14 days after the item is returned, subject to an inspection to ensure it meets our policies. Refunds are processed within 5 to 7 business days after we receive the returned item.

Can I exchange an item?

Yes, exchanges are possible within 14 days of delivery if the item is unused and in its original packaging. Please contact our support team to arrange an exchange.

Shipping

How long does shipping take?

Shipping times depend on your location and the shipping method chosen during purchase. While we strive to meet estimated delivery times, external factors like customs delays or carrier issues may affect them.

What shipping options are available?

We offer several shipping options, including standard and express shipping, depending on your location. Please check the available options during the purchase process for details on costs and delivery times.

Do you ship internationally?

We currently ship to most countries in the European Union. For other destinations, please contact our support team for a personalized quote.

Warranty

What warranties do you offer?

To request a warranty repair, please contact our support team with your order details and a description of the issue. We will guide you through the process.
