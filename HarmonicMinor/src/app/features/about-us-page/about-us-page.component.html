About Us

HarmonicMinor was founded in 2023 by Víctor Sánchez, a junior programmer with a deep passion for music and instruments. The idea was born from long nights of coding accompanied by the sounds of guitars, synthesizers, and coffee-fueled creativity.

As both a tech enthusiast and amateur musician, Víctor envisioned a platform that would bring together his two greatest interests — a space where technology meets sound. HarmonicMinor started as a solo project in a home studio, focused on building simple digital tools for independent musicians.

"To me, music and code are the same: a language of expression. HarmonicMinor is where these two worlds merge."

— Víctor Sánchez, Founder

Though the project is still young, HarmonicMinor has already helped dozens of musicians with tools like digital tuners, beat makers, and virtual pedalboards — all sold with passion and precision. The goal is not to become the biggest, but to always stay authentic and helpful to fellow music lovers.

Mission and Values

Our mission is to democratize access to quality musical instruments and foster a passion for music in people of all ages and levels. We believe that music has the power to transform lives, and we want to be the bridge that connects people with their musical potential.

Our vision is to become the national benchmark in the sale of musical instruments, recognized not only for the quality of our products, but also for our commitment to music education and support for emerging artists.

Passion

We love music and convey that passion in everything we do, from product selection to personalized advice.

Quality

We carefully select each product in our catalog, ensuring we offer only instruments and equipment that meet our quality standards.

Knowledge

Our team is made up of musicians and experts who can offer professional advice and answer any technical questions.

Community

We foster a vibrant musical community through events, workshops, and collaborations with local artists.
